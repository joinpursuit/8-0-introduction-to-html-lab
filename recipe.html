The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread on each other on a cutting board.
  2. Whith the butter knife, spread 1/8 layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side,spread 1/8 of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandiwch in half.
  6. Eat and Enjoy :)
