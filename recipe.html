The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both sides of bread next to each other on the cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a layer of peanubutter on one of the slices.
  3. On the other slice of bread, spread a layer of jelly.
  4. Put the two pieces of bread together.
  5. With the sharp knife, cute the sandwich in half.
