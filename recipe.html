The Perfect P&J

Description This peanut butter is my fav sandwich.

    Ingredients
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • A butter knife
      • Directions
  • Lay both sides of bread next to each other
  • Spread the peanut butter on the bread
  • Eat and enjoy
