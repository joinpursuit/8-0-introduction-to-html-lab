The Perfect PB&J


Description

PB & J is my favourite sandwich


Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slice of bread next to each other on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly
