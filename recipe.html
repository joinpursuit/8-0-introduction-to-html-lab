The Perfect PB&J

Description

A double layer peanut butter and jelly sandwich that you can share or enjoy yourself for 2 times the pleasure.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of Smucker's Concord Grape Jelly
  • 2 tablespoons of Skippy's Peanut Butter

Directions

  1. Spread 1 tablespoon of peanut butter onto a slice of bread.
  2. Top with 1 tablespoon of grape jelly.
  3. Repeat steps one and two on the second slice of bread.
  4. Layer third slice of bread between PB&J slices.
