Just Simple PB & J

Description

The simple and classic PB & J to fulfill your hunger. You looking for a quick meal to go here it is!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 knife
  • 1 plate

Directions

  1. On one slice of bread, spread peanut butter over the bread with knife.
  2. On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly evenly over the bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in.
  4. Serve & Enjoy!
  5. Optional: Remove crusts, cut diagonally.
