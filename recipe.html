Cheese Mango Recipe

Description

This recipe will guide the reader on how to make a chess infused Dominican style mangu from scratch. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Plantains
  • Meltable Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk
    •

    Directions

    1. Peel Plantains
    2. Cut peeled plantains in half, place in pot, add water until boil.
    3. Remove boiled plantains from water, place in bowl.
    4. Smash plantains while adding butter until soft
    5. Add a small amount of the boiled water to smashed plantains to make softer.
    6. Add a very small amount of milk to mix with plantains in order to increase softness.
    7. Finally, add desired amount of meltable cheese. Mix in cheese with smashed plantains until all is mixed together
