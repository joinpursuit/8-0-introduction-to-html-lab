The Perfect Bacon, Egg, and Cheese Sandwich

Description

A New York staple, commonly ordered in deli's or street cart's as a quick lunch. Personally my favorite sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • 2 slices of bacon.
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Heat up a frying pan
  2. Put two slices of bacon in the pan.
  3. When bacon is cooked, crack two eggs and add them to the pan.
  4. Add salt and pepper to the eggs.
  5. Once eggs are nearly finished, add two slices of cheese to the pan and fold everything together.
  6. Now put everything on bread and enjoy!
