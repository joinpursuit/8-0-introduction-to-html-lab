The Perfect Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is my favorite dessert. For more information, check out this similar recipe .

Ingredients

  • 1 Graham Cracker Crust
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Packs of Philadelphia Cream Cheese

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, vanilla with mixer until blended. Add eggs and beat until blended.
  3. Pour into crust.
  4. Bake 40 minutes or until center is almost set. Cool. Refrigerate 3 hours.
