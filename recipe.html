Tiramisu Recipe

Description

This tiramisu cake will make you want grab a slice right this second.

Ingredients

  • 250g mascarpone
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of Italian coffee (without sugar)
  • Â½ glass of marsala
  • Lady fingers biscuits
  • Cocoa powder

Directions

  1. Seperate the parts of the egg. Egg whites in one bowl and yolks in the another bowl.
  2. Take the white eggs bowl and whip it until it is quite firm.
  3. Whisk the yolks and the sugar until it changes colour and becomes pale.
  4. Add the mascarpone to the yolk and sugar combination. Mix well.
  5. Now gently fold the whipped egg whites into the rest of the mixture until combined. This is the cream for your tiramisu.
  6. Mix the Marsala into the coffee, and dip the lady fingers into the coffee for just a few seconds. You can now start building the tiramisu by placing the damp biscuits at the bottom of your serving glass.
  7. Cover the biscuits with a layer of the cream, then layer with more biscuits then some cream and some cacao powder. Keep repeating this until your glass is full, ensuring the top layer is cream.
  8. Finish your tiramisu with a light
  9. Refrigerate for a few hours before serving, or it can be kept in the fridge for up to 2 days!
