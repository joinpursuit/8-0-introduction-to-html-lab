Southern Red Rice and Sausage

Description

Red Rice & Sausage is a classic southern dish. Simple to make, with limited ingredients -perfect for lunches or dinners.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 1 lb sausage links cut into thin slices
  • 1 medium onion, medium dice
  • 1 bell pepper, medium dice
  • 3 C water, plus additional if needed
  • 4oz can tomato paste
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • Dash of Cayenne pepper to taste
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cook sausage until browned in a large pot over medium-high heat
  2. Add bell pepper, onion, and garlic, and cook until translucent - about 3 minutes
  3. Add butter and rice, cook until rice is slightly browned - about 3 minutes
  4. Slowly add water and remaining ingredients, stir thoroughly.
  5. Reduce heat to medium-low and cover. Let cook for 15 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes.
  6. Add small amounts of water as needed. Cook until rice is tender. Enjoy!
