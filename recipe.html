Pad Thai Recipe

Description

This is a Pad Thai recipe from Thailand Cuisine

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces flat rice noodles
  • 3 Tablespoons oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces uncooked shrimp, chicken, or extra-firm tofu, cut into small pieces
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 3 green onions, chopped
    • <1i> cup dry roasted peanuts
  • 2 lines
  • 1/2 cup Fresh cilantro, chopped

Directions

  1. Cook noodles according to package intructions, just till tender
  2. Mix the sauce ingredients together
  3. Heat one and half tablespoons of oil in a large saucepan
  4. Add the shrimp, chicken, or tofu, garlic and bell pepper. The shrimp will cook quickly
  5. Scramble the eggs and mix them in the pan
  6. Add noodles, sauce, bean sprouts, and peanuts to sauce pan
