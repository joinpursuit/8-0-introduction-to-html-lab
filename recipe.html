The Perfect PB&J

Description

The original, the O.G., the one you brought for lunch in a brown bag. The three-ingredient go-to that never goes out of style. Behold: The classic PB & J.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 Tablespoons SKIPPY® Creamy Peanut Butter
  • 2 Tablespoons WELCH'S® Concord Grape Jelly
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. On one slice of bread, spread peanut butter evenly over the bread.
  2. On the other slice of bread, spread the jelly evenly over the bread.
  3. Put the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing in.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
  5. Optional: Remove crusts, cut diagonally
