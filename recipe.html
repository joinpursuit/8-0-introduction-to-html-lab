Purple Sweet Potato Ice Cream!

Description

If you love sweet potato ice cream then this is the recipe for you. As a bonus it's Purple!! How cool is that!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups purple sweet potato (about 3 sweeet potatoes)
  • 1 1/3 cups whole milk
  • 5 egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/3 cups heavy cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Ice, as needed

Directions

  1. Roast sweet potatoes in 400 degrees farenheit until tender, about an hour
  2. Peel sweet potatoes and run them through a ricer or food mill. (This should yield approximately 1 1/3 cups mashed sweet potato.)
  3. In a medium saucepan, bring milk to a simmer, then remove from heat.
  4. While milk is heating, whisk together egg yolks and sugar for 3-4 minutes, until egg yolks lighten in color.
  5. Temper the egg yolks with hot milk by continously whisking yolks and adding the hot milk a little bit at a time, slowly raising the temperature of the yolks so they don't curdle. Return the egg and milk mixture back to the saucepan over low heat, continually mixing until it reaches 170 degrees farenheit, or the mixture coats the back of a spoon and holds a line, then remove from heat
  6. In a large mixing bowl, add enough ice and water to nest a smaller mixing bowl inside without it overflowing.
  7. Mix sweet potatoes, heavy cream and salt into egg mixture to incorporate. Run ice cream base through a strainer into the nested bowl in the ice. Stir ice cream base to chill it as fast as possible and get it as cold as possible before pouring into your ice cream machine.
  8. Freeze chilled ice cream base according to directions for your ice cream machine.
