The Best Ham Sandwich!

Description

This is the simplest sandwich to make and im still too lazy to make it!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 4 slices whole wheat bread
  • 4 ounces thinly sliced deli ham
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 2 ounces smoked cheddar cheese, sliced

Directions

  1. Spread mayo over two slices of bread.
  2. Layer each with cheese, tomato, ham and lettuce.
  3. Top with remaining bread.
