The Perfect PB&J

Description of Sandwich

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Visit here for the recipe as well

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of any bread
  • 1 jar of any jelly
  • 1 jar of any peanut butter (could also be any other nut butter)
  • A butter knife
  • A plate
  • A table

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on the plate
  2. With the butter knife, spread a layer of peanut butter (or alternative) on one slice of bread
  3. On the other slice of bread, spread an equal layer of jelly
  4. Carefully place the two slices together
  5. Optional: Cut the sandwich in half for sharing
