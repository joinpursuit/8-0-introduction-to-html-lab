Tabitha Brown Grilled Cheese!

Description

Add some pizzazz to your regular grilled cheese, with a bit of veggies and seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • As much shredded cheese as you want
  • A handful of minced Baby Spinach
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder

Directions

  1. Spread 2 slices of bread with butter, then sprinkle each with some chopped fresh spinach and a little shredded cheese.
  2. Top with a generous sprinkle of garlic powder.
  3. Place one slice of the prepared bread, cheese-side-down, into a preheated skillet and pan fry, moving the piece around in the pan a bit so it wonâ€™t stick, until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Scatter more spinach, cheese and garlic powder over the bread, then top it with the second slice of bread with the buttered side up.
  5. Flip the whole sandwich, cover the pan and cook a few minutes until the cheese is melted and the sandwich crusty and golden.
