The Perfect PB&J

Description

The Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich is not my favorite. However, when you add banana, honey and toast it it creates an amazing sandwich.

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A banana
  • Pure Honey/Agave
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board
  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spead a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter
  3. With the butter knife, spead a 1/8 inch layer of jelly
  4. Thinly cut banana and place on your peanut butter side
  5. Drizzle your honey/agave onto the bananas
  6. Carefully place two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  7. Toast sandwich
  8. With the sharp knife, carefully cut sandwich in half
  9. Enjoy!
