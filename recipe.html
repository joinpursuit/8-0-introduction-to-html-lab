The Perfect Potato Salad

Ingredients

Directions

A cajun zesty crunchy potato salad

  • potato medley
  • celery
  • onions
  • scallions
  • yellow mustard
  • miracle whip
  • honey
  • cajun seasoning
  • hardboiled egg
  • salt and pepper (for taste)
  1. boil your potatoes and eggs for 25 minutes until forktender
  2. drain into bowl and pour cold water to stop cooking
  3. peel eggs and cut potatos into small bite sized pieces
  4. dice up all veggies and add to dry mix with potatoes
  5. combine all wet ingredients (mustard, miracle whip, honey with seasonings
  6. pour mix in with dry ingredients
  7. lightly fold until combined
  8. ENJOY
