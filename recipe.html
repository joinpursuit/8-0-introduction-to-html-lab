The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. That's a lie, I prefer grilled cheese, but I need to do this for a school lab.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 1 jar of strawberry jam
  • 1 jar of chunky almond butter
  • A butter knife (for the nut butter obv)
  • A sharp knife (in case the jam fights back)
  • A cutting board (for the final pb&j&sacrifice)

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to eachother on your cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a layer of your nut butter of choice on one slice of bread.
  3. On the other naked slice of bread, spread a layer of your jam choice to your desired thiccness.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together being very careful as they are explosive if combined too quickly.
  5. With the sharp knife, it's time to vanquish this delicious sandwich once and for all by slicing it down the middle to create 2 triangle shaped pieces.
