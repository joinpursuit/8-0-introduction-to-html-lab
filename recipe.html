Penne Al La Vodka

Document

Description

Penne al la Vodka is by far my favorite savory meal. Made with quality ingredients, this comes out beautiful.

Ingredients

  • Penne pasta
  • Proscuitto
  • Shallot
  • Vodka
  • Cream
  • Marinara
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper flakes

Directions

  1. In a hot pan add in your proscuitto and shallot till the shallots are translucent
  2. Once translucent add one shot of vodka
  3. Add in your marinara and cream
  4. Basil and pepper flakes
  5. Then salt to desired taste
