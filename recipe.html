The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sanwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife,spread ans 1/8 layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. On right side,spread an1/8 layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top
  5. With the sharp knife,carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Proceed to enjoy PB&J!
