The Perfect Baked Ziti

Description

This perfect baked ziti is my favorite meal. It has the perfect balance of cheese and sauce and looks spectacular fresh out the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ziti or penne pasta
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound fresh chicken, ground beef, or ground pork
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh rosemary or basil
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 4 cups jarred marinara or pasta sauce, or make your own tomato sauce
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan or pecorino cheese
  • 1 heaping cup ricotta cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1 Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
  2. Step 2 In a large skillet, brown onion and ground beef over medium heat. Add spaghetti sauce, and simmer 15 minutes.
  3. Step 3 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13 inch baking dish. Layer as follows: 1/2 of the ziti, Provolone cheese, sour cream, 1/2 sauce mixture, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese and remaining sauce mixture. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Step 4 Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until cheeses are melted.
