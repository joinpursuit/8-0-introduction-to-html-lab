The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is awesome. It's my favorite night time snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of wheat bread
  • 1 jar of strawberry jam
  • 1 jar of crunchy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread on cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread layer of peanut butter on bread on the left.
  3. On the right side, spread layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place two halves together with the jelly on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
