The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is the perfect sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
  6. Bask in the glory of the sandwich. Take pictures. Send them twice, no thrice, to all your friends. Make them jelly.
  7. Luxuriate in the sweet and nutty flavors, merging together, reanimating your taste buds.
