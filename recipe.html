The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanut butter and jelly sandwhichis my favorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two luves together so that the jelly is on the top.
  5. With the sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwhiches in half.
  6. Then eat the sandwhich.
