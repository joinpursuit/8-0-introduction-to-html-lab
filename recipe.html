The Perfect PB & Jelly

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A sharp Knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. with the butter knife,spread 1/8 inch layer of PB on left side
  3. On the right side, spread 1/8 layer of jelly
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that jelly is on top
  5. with the sharp knife, cut the sandwich in half.
